Online ticketing / booking is a convenient way of booking your travel over the internet. Using PIA online services you can:

Search flights - Find a suitable flight/routing/date Select flights - Display of fares and selection of suitable fares
Seat Selection – Select normal free seat or purchase bulk head seat (if required)
Passengers & Payments – Insert details of passengers and payment card details on a secured web-page
Confirmation – Generation of confirmation of the web booking.
