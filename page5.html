Work from home ...Also while travelling...Doesn't seems excited!!

Mr. Sahban Tariq | Nov 21,2021 | 170 comments | 15 min read

Ever since the pandemic hit, the media has been awash with trend watching pieces about how society might change. Some of these virus induced trends will surely fade over time, but one I believe will stick is the remote work revolution.
Companies around the world have been forced to adopt flexible work from home rules.The opportunities to combine travel and work have never been so widespread
If you want to experiment with being a digital nomad of sorts-even for a week or few days-now could be a perfect time.
It is been fascinating to watch this unfold and seeing people adapt to this different way of working.

All you need is just laptop and internet connection, then you can potentially work from anywhere.

I love working while travelling- I do it all the time! I manage my site from pretty much anywhere, which can be my home, a cafe in my local neighborhood, or from some far-flung location. I've also been on dedicated remote work trips, where the whole point is to work somewhere in an inspiring location, either by myself or other freelancers or remote workers.

You might think that I'm destroying the meaning of travel because I'm still working.

Dudes!! Think it in another way.

A remote work trip will give u an amazing chance to break from routine, to enjoy a change the beauty of scenery, spending lunchtime and evenings in nature, surfing or chilling in hammock.

Even a smaller change in atmosphere brings positivity in one, freshes his mind and body and allow exciting ideas to come towards him.
